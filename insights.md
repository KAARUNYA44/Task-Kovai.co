# Actionable Insights from Transport Forecasting

## 1. Increase Bus Frequency on Local Routes
**Insight:** Local Route demand is expected to rise by ~12% in the next week.  
**Action:** Schedule additional buses during peak hours to avoid passenger overflow.

---

## 2. Rapid Route Usage Surpassing Light Rail
**Insight:** Rapid Route shows stronger growth than Light Rail.  
**Action:** Reallocate resources such as drivers or maintenance schedules to Rapid Routes.

---

## 3. Stable School Transport Demand
**Insight:** School ridership remains stable with no major spikes expected.  
**Action:** Continue current scheduling — no additional buses required.

---

## 4. Midweek (Wed–Fri) Service Peaks
**Insight:** Peak Service demand consistently spikes midweek.  
**Action:** Prepare backup fleet and adjust driver rosters accordingly.

---

## 5. Long-Term Investment Shift
**Insight:** Continuous rise in Rapid Route ridership suggests shifting commuter preference.  
**Action:** Plan infrastructure upgrades and route expansions for Rapid services.
